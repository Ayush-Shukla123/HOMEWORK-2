HAMBURGER🍔

A Hamburger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat,usually beaf,placed inside a sliced bread-roll or bun.The patty may be pan fried ,griled,smoked or flame broil. Hamburgers are ofen served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon; condiments such as- ketch-up, mustard, mayonnaise.and are frequently, placed on sesame seed buns. A Hamburger topped with cheese is called Cheese Hamburger. 🍔
